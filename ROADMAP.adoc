This roadmap tracks OikosBot, the ecological and economic code-analysis app.
The Oikos economics accounting DSL now lives in
https://github.com/hyperpolymath/oikos-economics-accounting-dsl[`hyperpolymath/oikos-economics-accounting-dsl`].

Status legend: `done` | `in-progress` | `blocked` | `planned`

== v0.1 - Scaffold

*Status:* in-progress

* [x] AffineScript webhook receiver scaffold at `bot-integration-affine/`
* [x] GitHub App manifest at `.github/app.yml`
* [x] Policy rules in Datalog and DeepProbLog
